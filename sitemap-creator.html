Where to Upload Your Sitemap

Once you've generated and downloaded your sitemap, upload it to the root directory of your website (e.g., https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml). Then, submit it to Google via Google Search Console and to Bing via Bing Webmaster Tools to help search engines discover and index your pages.

Benefits of a Sitemap

  • Improves website indexing by search engines, especially for new websites or updates.
  • Helps search engines discover all pages, including those that are not well-linked.
  • Provides metadata about each URL, helping search engines prioritize important pages.
  • Can improve visibility and organic search performance.
